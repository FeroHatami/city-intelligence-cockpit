# City Intelligence Cockpit

## Goal

Create a 2D/3D city intelligence cockpit that gives a clear overview of the city, buildings, businesses, offices, shops, suppliers, public places, and opportunities.

## First City

Munich, Germany.

## Main Use Case

Use the city map as an opportunity radar:
- find businesses
- filter by category
- inspect buildings and locations
- save interesting places
- score business opportunities with AI

## First Version

The first version should be simple:

1. Interactive 2D/3D map of Munich
2. Filters for business categories
3. Clickable places/buildings
4. Save places as leads
5. AI opportunity score
6. Simple dashboard/sidebar

## Recommended Base

Start from an open-source geospatial platform instead of building from scratch.

Preferred base:
- City Intelligence Cockpit geospatial prototype foundation

Alternative later:
- MapLibre GL JS
- deck.gl
- CesiumJS
